CLEANTheory was founded on the science of chlorine dioxide chemistry — engineered to protect the environments where indoor agriculture thrives.

Our Mission

CLEANTheory exists to bring EPA-registered chlorine dioxide solutions to indoor agriculture as a fully managed service. We believe that clean growing environments should not require growers to become chemists — so we handle the science while you focus on cultivation.

We bridge the gap between advanced chemistry and real-world cultivation. Our team combines expertise in ClO₂ formulation, facility sanitation protocols, and agricultural science to deliver turnkey protection that scales with your operation.

Scientific Rigor
