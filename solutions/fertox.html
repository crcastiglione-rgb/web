The Problem

Irrigation and fertigation lines are breeding grounds for biofilm, Pythium, Fusarium, and bacterial buildup. These contaminants clog emitters, reduce nutrient uptake, and spread disease throughout your facility — silently reducing yield and quality.

Traditional treatments like chlorine bleach and hydrogen peroxide degrade quickly, fail in biofilm, and can damage root systems. Growers need a solution that works continuously without compromising plant health.

How FERTox Works

FERTox integrates EPA-registered chlorine dioxide directly into your irrigation and fertigation systems. Applied at precise, crop-safe concentrations, ClO₂ eliminates pathogens, dissolves biofilm, and prevents mineral scale — all while preserving nutrient integrity.

Biofilm Elimination

ClO₂ penetrates and dissolves biofilm that coats the inside of irrigation lines, emitters, and reservoirs — something chlorine bleach simply cannot do.

Pathogen Kill

Achieves near-complete elimination of Pythium, Fusarium, Botrytis, E. coli, and other waterborne threats at crop-safe dosing levels.

Root-Zone Safe

Unlike harsh chemicals, ClO₂ at managed concentrations protects roots and beneficial microbes while eliminating harmful organisms.

Managed & Monitored

CLEANTheory installs, calibrates, and continuously monitors your FERTox system. We handle the chemistry so you don't have to.

Scale Prevention

ClO₂ prevents calcium and mineral scale buildup in drip lines and emitters, maintaining uniform flow and nutrient distribution.

EPA Registered

All FERTox products are EPA-registered for agricultural use. Full compliance documentation is included with every deployment.
