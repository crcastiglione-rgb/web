The Problem

Surfaces in indoor agriculture facilities harbor Botrytis, Powdery Mildew, Fusarium, Aspergillus, and a host of other pathogens. Between crop cycles — and even during active grows — these organisms colonize floors, walls, tables, trays, tools, and HVAC housings.

Standard cleaning agents like quaternary ammonium compounds (quats) and bleach leave residues, corrode equipment, and miss organisms protected in micro-biofilms on textured surfaces. Incomplete sanitation between cycles is the #1 vector for recurring contamination.
